"Transcendence: What It Is and Why It Matters"

March 31st 2023 2 Min read

This article explores the concept of transcendence, defined as the ability to rise above one's current state and achieve a higher level of existence. It discusses the importance of transcendence in personal growth and spiritual development, as well as its potential benefits for mental health and well-being.



References:
"Transcendence and Mental Health: The Benefits of Spiritual Experiences," by Janae B. Weinhold, PhD and Barry K. Weinhold, PhD, Psychology Today
